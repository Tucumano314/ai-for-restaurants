Self‑Service AI Tools

Do it yourself with our curated list of AI tools. These apps empower restaurateurs to automate operations without hiring a developer.

Reservation & Table Management

ResyOS (training), OpenTable (OpenTable Academy), Tock and Hostme.

Menu Design

Canva’s Menu Maker (tutorial), Venngage AI Menu Generator, Designs.ai Menu Maker, and Appy Pie.

Logo & Branding

Looka, LogoAI, Logomakerr.ai, Placeit, and Zarla.

Marketing & Content

Jasper.ai, Copy.ai, Canva Magic Write, and other AI copywriters to craft social posts, email campaigns and more. Check out this marketing AI tutorial for inspiration.

Chatbots & Voice Assistants

Build conversational experiences with Dialogflow, Twilio Voice/IVR, and Tidio. Watch this chatbot tutorial to get started.

Analytics & Reporting

Analyze operations with Tableau for Restaurants, Microsoft Power BI, and Fourth Analytics. Learn how to build your own dashboard with this reporting tutorial.

Inventory Management

Simplify ordering with MarketMan, BlueCart, and Toast Inventory Management. Watch this inventory tutorial on reducing waste.

Scheduling & HR

Manage teams with 7shifts, Homebase, and When I Work. This staff scheduling tutorial explains best practices.
